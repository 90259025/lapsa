Lapsa: Easy, Elegant Slides

Lapsa is the incredibly simple way to build beautiful and interactive presentations. It uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for its layout and functionality, meaning easy things are easy and nearly anything is possible.

Use the arrow keys (or enter or space) or tap with two fingers to move forward.

Hassle-Free Navigation

Move the cursor to the left edge or tap with three fingers to open the shelf, which contains more navigation options.

Single arrows move a build at a time, double arrows move a whole slide at a time, and the middle button opens the table view, which adapts to fit any screen size.
